BENEFITS

It Reduces Stress and Anxiety Massage is proven to reduce stress and anxiety, which is a huge plus for any dog and especially helpful for pups that are easily stressed or worried.
Improves Circulation A healthy circulatory system brings muscles the oxygen they need for optimal functioning. Dogs love to run and play, so give yours a massage before exercising to improve blood flow. This is also a way to reduce potential muscle cramps. You wouldn’t exercise yourself without warming up, right?
Relieves Joint Pain When your dog reaches a more mature age, they’re likely to have some joint pain and may develop arthritis. Add to that the fact that some breeds are prone to having joint issues such as hip dysplasia. A gentle rub down of their muscles will make all the difference in their joint pain and function.
Promotes Deep Breathing A soothing massage causes breathing to slow down and deepen. Not only does breathing deeply promote a feeling of relaxation, but it supports healthy lung function for your pup.
Helps Prevents Injury Whether it’s before or after play time, a nice massage can help prevent muscle and joint injury. Using massages to get Fido’s limbs conditioned for a good run and rubbing down the sore spots afterwards is always a good idea.
Supports Well-Being Finally, one of the benefits of animal massage is that it supports overall well-being. In other words, it has the effect of restoring balance and energy in the body. You’re apt to see your fur baby enjoying their day much more after a morning massage!
